Event Description

A beginning is a chance to start fresh. It allows the chance to imagine, to create, and to reshape the future. From personal journeys to global challenges, “New Beginnings” invites us to reflect on the ways change sparks growth and innovation. As our school embraces our founding year, Reddam House North Shore itself embodies a new beginning - a community that begins to share its identity, traditions, and future. At TEDx Reddam House North Shore Youth, speakers will share bold ideas that explore what it means to start again - whether it’s confronting social issues, rethinking technology, embracing creativity, or building a more sustainable world. Together, we’ll celebrate the courage it takes to turn endings into opportunities and to transform challenges into possibilities. As our school hosts its inaugural TEDxYouth, this event is more than just a showcase of talks - it is the beginning of a tradition. A tradition of curiosity, community, and conversation that inspires us all to think individually and act decisively. We're shaping an adventure to embrace individuality and prepare leaders for the world. We are proud to deliver an event run by students, for students.
